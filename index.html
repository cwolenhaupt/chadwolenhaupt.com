About

Chad Wolenhaupt is an author, retired founder, and builder in the second half of life. After selling three businesses, he writes about the places where achievement stops answering the deeper questions: work, fatherhood, faith, discipline, drift, and the cost of living by momentum. He lives in Franklin, Tennessee with his wife, Michele.
