About

Chad Wolenhaupt is an author, retired founder, and builder calling people toward a more intentional life. After building and selling three businesses, he began speaking to the gap many successful people know but rarely name: the distance between what they have achieved and who they are becoming. His work challenges people to rebuild the inner life, strengthen the home, recover discipline, and live with legacy in view. He lives in Franklin, Tennessee with his wife, Michele.
